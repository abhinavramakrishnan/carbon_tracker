How does it work ?

PowerPal uses data such as the wattage of appliances, the number of hours they are used, and the cost of electricity to estimate the amount of energy consumed and the associated costs. PowerPal also provides recommendations for reducing energy usage and cost. You can input information about your household's energy consumption to get a better understanding of your electricity usage and costs.

Ask Terra

Terra is an intelligent virtual assistant designed to provide you with tips and strategies to reduce your energy consumption. You can interact with Terra once you login. Whether you're looking to reduce your electricity bill or decrease your carbon footprint, Terra is an excellent resource to help you achieve your energy-saving goals.
